How Summarization works



Algorithm

  1. Eliminate stopwords(common words like is, the,etc) and punctuations.
  2. Identify most appearing word using max(count).
  3. Assign weights to each word by normalizing with highest appearing word.
  4. Assign weights to each sentence by adding the weights of each word in the sentence.
  5. Sort the sentences by weight in descending order.
  6. Pick first n sentences as summarized text.
  7. Join the sentences together to form final summarized text.
